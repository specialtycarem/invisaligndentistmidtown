What is Invisalign?

Invisalign is a modern orthodontic treatment that uses a series of custom-made, removable Invisalign Clear Aligners to gradually shift your teeth into the desired position. Unlike traditional braces, these nearly invisible trays fit snugly over your teeth and can be removed while eating, drinking, or brushing — giving you the freedom to live your life without compromise.
